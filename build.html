Glossary

Skill Floor

How easy the build is to pick up and use effectively with minimal practice

Skill Ceiling

The skill level needed to master this build

Single-Target

Effectiveness against 1 enemy (Bossing potential)

Multi-Target

Effectiveness against multiple enemies (Mobbing potential)

Normal, Hard, Nightscream & Topaz denote the minimum difficulty required for a Souljade to drop. With Topaz meaning it only drops from Enigma Domain or other missions that can drop all Souljades (i.e. Tempered Inferno • Nightscream).
