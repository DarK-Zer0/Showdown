BUILDS

The following builds are listed with no particular order in mind aside from their main damage source. In terms of damage output, right now Ice builds are at the top, followed by Bow builds.
Difficulty denotes how hard a build to use it effectively, while Power denotes the DPS ceiling in respect to other builds.

Color Coded Names
Each Souljade's name will have a color assigned to it that tells the minimum difficulty of the missions that can drop it (including Nirriti's Ruins).

White = Commemorative Souljade (buy from the Narakapedia).
Light Blue = Normal and above difficulty.
Purple = Hard and above difficulty.
Red = Nightscream difficulty.
Brown = Enigma Domain.

Description

Your attacks will build up Frostbite and Toxin on enemies, which will explode into an Ice Blasts and Poison Blasts respectively once certain thresholds are met. Ice Blasts out of Frost Surge will attempt to freeze you. You can defuse this freeze attempt or use any ability that applies golden focus to yourself, such as Justina's Skill, Shadowswipe.

The freeze attempt will activate Frostbite Surge, preventing you from accumulating Frostbite on yourself for 25 seconds and allowing you to summon Ice Thorns whenever you use your Hero's Skill (this has a 4-second cooldown).

After enough Ice damage instances, you will charge up an extremely strong attack known as Chain Crush. You will see a symbol appear in the top left that indicates it is ready to be used. To activate it, use any uncharged melee attack.

[Moderation] +100% is necessary for this build to be effective.

Recommended Heroes

Viper Ning, Feria Shen, Tianhai, Tarka Ji,

Description

Deal massive damage by using the Stage 2 Charged Attack of your Bow. Perfectly timing the release of the charge, while Deadeye is active, will cast Void Destroyer, a massive 3-part attack that only consumes 1 ammo. Beware, this build will leave you at 1 Health, so you'll mainly rely on your Armor to survive any hits.

Reaching ~8.5% Deadeye build rate will allow you to activate Deadeye with just 3 Stage 2 Charged Attacks instead of 4. 30.5%+ Deadeye build rate will allow you to activate Deadeye in 2 Stage 2 Charged Attacks if they're headshots. 43.9%+ Deadeye build rate will allow you to activate Deadeye in 2 Stage 2 Charged Attacks as long as one is a headshot.

Description

Poison Blasts activate Noxious Blast, which applies 1 stack of [Toxified] to the enemy, and Pestilent Pool, which generates a poison pool that applies up to 2 [Toxified] stacks to enemies standing within it.

Standing in the pool for 2 seconds inflicts [Toxified] on yourself, allowing you to transfer 2 [Toxified] stacks to the enemy by using a Martial Skill or the 3rd attack in any uncharged attack combo (excluding Greatsword and Polesword) via Infectious Touch.

Furthermore, this will activate Toxin Surge, which prevents you from being afflicted with [Toxified] for 15 seconds and allows you to apply an additional stack of [Toxified] whenever you use a Martial Skill.

When an enemy reaches 6 stacks of [Toxified], Toxin Blast activates, dealing 48x Attack damage to the surrounding area. When you have applied [Toxified] to enemies 11 times, Chain Crush will be ready, adding a 22.4x Poison Blast DMG attack to your next uncharged melee attack.

Recommended Heroes

Target Attributes

Placeholder Attributes

Deadeye build rate

Description

A slow starting build that requires you to activate Deadeye by hitting your shots on enemies. Counterstrikes give significantly more Deadeye charge (almost 3x the amount) and headshots give slightly more (20% more), so good aim is heavily rewarded. Headshots will deal greatly increased damage with this build, so it is heavily advised to aim for the head. When shooting a weakpoint or head while in the air and Deadeye is active, you will backflip and hover in the air for a short period and can fire a powerful shot. This will make it easier to hit headshots from safety, with the angle you backflip dependent on the angle you hit the enemy. Not recommended for console players due to the difficulty of aiming.

Recommended Heroes

Feria Shen, Valda Cui & Kylin Zhang
