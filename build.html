Jade Set Ups

This section will showcase the standard Souljade setups for each damage type, including their respective strengths and weaknesses. Ensure you replace the (Ice/Thunder)-Wielding Souljade with the one corresponding to your hero's favored element. See Element Jades for more info. In terms of damage output, right now Burn builds are at the top, followed by Thunder builds, and lastly Ice builds.

The following builds are listed with no particular order in mind aside from their main damage source. Difficulty does not denote how strong a build is, but rather how hard it is to use it effectively.

Description

This build relies heavily on the [Scorching] Rare Attribute for its damage output. It makes use of Thunderpull, Thunder Counter, Thunderproof, and 0 Thunder-Wielding to counter Divine Thunder frequently, allowing for repeated activation of [Scorching]. Combined with Draco Stride, this converts into Hydroflare damage for massive bursts of damage. Currently, this build has the highest damage output in the game, but it requires heavy investment to use.

[Scorching] +9 or higher is recommended for optimal performance.
Counter triggers Recovery recommended to prevent having to stop for heals over extended fights.

Defuse • Flaming Blade and Defuse • Raging Flame give you access to powerful Burns when you Defuse an attack. Draco Stride then Soaks the enemy to convert those Burns to Hydroflare for a big burst of damage. Defuse triggers Recovery +200 or higher recommended for safe use.

Frost Lotus

Wielding Type

Ice-Wielding

Target Attributes

x4 [Moderation], x8 [Windfall], x4 Ice Blast DMG, x4 Attack
or
x12 [Windfall], x4 Attack, x4 Ice Blast DMG

Optional Attributes

Increase DMG to Boss & Melee Weapon DMG.

Description

Attack enemies to build Frostbite on them and activate Ice Blasts for massive damage. Successive Ice Blast increases the number of times Ice Blast hits, multiplying the damage it deals by 1.65 as well as increasing the number of Lotus Flowers spawned from 1 to 3. Frozen Blast, if chosen, reduces the cooldown of Ice Blast from 6s to 4s. Sub-Zero Seal will build Frostbite on you over time, but you can avoid being frozen by timing your ability or Ultimate at the right moment. Tundra Might allows you to activate Ice Blasts significantly faster. If you use Arcane Thunder, you can proc Ice Blasts on distant mobs and adds extra damage to your ability casts.

[Moderation] +100% turns this into a powerful Mod build.

Toxic Lotus

Wielding Type

Poison-Wielding

Target Attributes

x4 [Moderation], x8 [Windfall], x4 Poison Blast DMG, x4 Attack
or
x12 [Windfall], x4 Poison Blast DMG, x4 Attack

Optional Attributes

Increase DMG to Boss & Melee Weapon DMG.

Description

By using the [Moderation] Rare Attribute, you can reduce the cooldown of your ability whenever you activate a Poison Blast by attacking enemies. Poison Blasts activate Noxious Blast which applies 1 stack of [Toxified] to the enemy. It also activates Pestilent Pool which generates up to 2 [Toxified] stacks as they stand in the poison pool that's generated. Standing in the pool for 2 seconds will apply [Toxified] to yourself, which you can apply 2 [Toxified] stacks to the enemy by using your Martial Skill thanks to Infectious Touch, Defuse Counterattack or 3rd attack in any uncharged attack combo (doesn't apply to Greatsword or Polesword). Once an enemy reaches 6 stacks of [Toxified], Toxin Blast will activate dealing major damage to the surrounding area. Every stack of Toxified applied to enemies will summon a Lotus Flower.

[Moderation] +75% or higher recommended if pursuing a Mod version of this build.

Element Jades

With so many choices in Souljades and Potential Plans, it can be hard to know what build to go with. This section will help for choosing the most optimal Element Souljades as well as what Potential Plans they best combo with. Souljades with "• Element" in their name gain bonus elemental damage for that corresponding type of Wielding they have, making it so you're able to activate Ice Blasts, Divine Thunder or Poison Blasts faster on enemies using the special attacks they give. They also generally have better DPS than their base counterpart.
