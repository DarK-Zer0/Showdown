What is Showdown?

Showdown is a PvE game mode in Naraka Bladepoint also referred to as its Campaign mode. Teams of up to 3 can face off against various enemies and bosses through varying levels of difficulty. It can be great for players who are just starting Naraka as well as veterans seeking to mix things up. Progression you make in this game mode however does not contribute to PvP and vice versa. There are also rewards for playing it, such as its own Game Pass, Exclusive Badges & Titles, and Limited Time Events.

To learn more, click one of the tabs from the menu up top.
