				<h1>Element Jades</h1>
				<p class="center">With so many choices in Souljades and Potential Plans, it can be hard to know what build to go with.
					This section will help for choosing the most optimal Element Souljades as well as what Potential Plans they best combo with.
					Souljades with "• Element" in their name gain bonus elemental damage for that corresponding type of Wielding they have, 
					making it so you're able to activate Ice Blasts, Divine Thunder or Poison Blasts faster on enemies using the special attacks they give. 
					They also generally have better DPS than their base counterpart.
				</p>
